Frequently Asked Questions

What is StudIQ?

StudIQ is a smart study companion app that helps students learn more effectively with features like flashcards, quizzes, notes, reminders, and more—all in one place.

Can I create my own flashcards in StudIQ?

Absolutely! You can create, edit, and organize your own flashcard decks. You can also access pre-made sets shared by other users.

Will there be handwriting or voice note features?

We're working on it! Handwriting input and voice notes are planned for future updates.
